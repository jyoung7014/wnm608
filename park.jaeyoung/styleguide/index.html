RV to Me is an online marketplace that connects people who want to earn extra income by renting out their recreational vehicles with people who want to go to RV trips for the flexibilities on diverse considerations of accommodations and outdoor activities.

Typical lodgings always present limitations for people who have certain physical restriction or circumstances. This mobile application is made for a marketplace not only to introduce a new concepts of accommodation for people who want more flexibility on where they stay, but also to give a chance to earn extra money from people who have their own RVs.

This distinct service will:

+ Provide more options of accommodation for people who are looking for more flexibility on policies of where they stay.

+ Build customer to customer(C2C) marketplace to draw benefits both sides.

+ Provide feasible idea of making extra income for RV owners.
